Simulate steady-state natural convection of air inside a 2D square enclosure with a hot horizontal cylinder in the centre. Outer enclosure is 1 m × 1 m. Inner cylinder has radius 0.1 m, centred at (0.5, 0.5), held at T = 350 K. The four outer walls are held at T = 300 K. Front and back are empty (2D). Gravity acts in the −y direction at 9.81 m/s². No inlet, no outlet — flow is driven entirely by buoyancy. Use buoyantBoussinesqSimpleFoam with kOmegaSST turbulence (5 % intensity, mixing length 0.07 m). Fluid: air with β = 3.4e-3 1/K, ν = 1.5e-5 m²/s, Pr = 0.7, T_ref = 300 K. Run for 3000 iterations with convergence criteria 1e-5, writing every 200 iterations.
